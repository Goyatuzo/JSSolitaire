People for the Ethical Treatment of Gingers (PETGingers) is the largest Ginger rights organization in the world, with more than twelve members and supporters worldwide.


PETGingers focuses its attention on the four areas in which the largest numbers of Gingers suffer the most intensely for the longest periods of time: on factory farms, in the clothing trade, in laboratories, and in the entertainment industry. We also work on a variety of other projects, including Gingineers without Borders as well as ending cruelty to domesticated Gingers.


PETGingers works through public education, cruelty investigations, research, legislation, special events, celebrity involvement, and protest campaigns.
